# Email template for participant invitation for mapping and interviews: Long

-------

Subject: Take part in the Discovering Hidden Community Leaders project – community mapping and interviews

Dear SSI Fellows,

The [***Discovering Hidden Community Leaders***](https://discourse-network.github.io/projects/1-30-discovering-leaders/) project (funded by DisCouRSE), is a collaboration between the University of Southampton, RCM Cooperative and the Software Sustainability Institute. The project aims to build a deeper understanding of the scope, value, and impact of Research Community Management within the research ecosystem.

**We need your help!**

We are looking to recruit SSI Fellows to contribute to mapping community-focused work and have the opportunity to participate in in-depth interviews.

Our mapping is similar to the information collected regularly by SSI as part of your fellowship but includes more details to better understand your involvement in different communities and activities.

We expect the form will take no more than 10 minutes to complete.

Are you also interested in being interviewed? Please indicate on the mapping form.

Our interviews will focus on experiences of research community management, including activities, recognition, and impact with the research ecosystem. You don’t need to have formally held a position as a research community manager, but you do need to have done community-focused work, voluntary or paid, as part of your career.

We are looking to recruit a maximum of 20 people - if we have more than 20 applicants, there will be a selection based on capturing a broad range of communities, domains, sectors, and career stages.

Interviews will be online for up to 1 hour and will take place in June 2026.

If selected, you will be contacted by a member of our team to set up an interview date.
